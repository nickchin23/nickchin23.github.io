About Me

I am a recent graduate of Rutgers University in New Brunswick/Piscataway New Jersey. I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Information Technology and Informatics with a minor in Mathematics.

Having minored in Mathematics, I am driven by problem solving. By using problem solving skills, I can go about a problem in many different ways get to an answer. If one way doesn't work, I would try again with another. The challenge of problem solving is what makes it enjoyable.

Using my problem solving skills with my degree I am able to excel within my job. Instead of just giving up when I run into problems I can do three things. 1. I can figure out what I did wrong and correct it, 2. try fix it using another method, or 3. ask someone to help me out the problem. I currently use this method of problem solving at my part time retail job. When I can't find an item for a shipping order, I would try to figure it out my self, look for it somewhere else, or ask a coworker for help. This method has helped me increase my store's sales.

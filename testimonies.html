We have all these questions and rightfully so, Doctor Wu takes the time to know her patients and she makes you fell at ease and worth listening to your complaint. You talk about where your pain is and even if you don't know where your pain is, she finds it. Amazing!

So you start your session with a _______, and an open mind still not sure what to expect. Secretly hoping that this _____ to help. You fell happy, bright, light, you feel positive, the tarnish is gone and you now SHINE.

Wellness and balance you feel it... but everybody see's it, in your eyes, on your face, in your walk, in YOU.
